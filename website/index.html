    A novel mobile full-screen ads library

FEATURES

AdsProphet is a new mobile ads library that solves the problem of "dead time" that occurs when network data is loading. AdsProphet finds and takes advantage of these small but valuable windows to deliver full-screen ads, which deliver a 25x greater CTR than banner ads, boosting your revenue.

Novel Prediction Algorithm

AdsProphet uses hierarchical user-perceived delay prediction algorithms to effeiciently and accurately estimate the user's waiting time for network operations.

More Ad Revenue

AdsProphet automatically finds opportunities to play full-screen ads, which have a 25 times greater Click-Through Rate (CTR) than banner ads.

Doesn't Compete with Existing Libraries

AdsProphet works side by side with your existing libraries to maximize revenue from your mobile app.

Sponsored by NSF I-Corps Program

AdsProphet is a start-up from Northwestern University's EECS Department. AdsProphet was selected by the NSF I-Corps Program to participate in its entrepreneurship program.
